What is a CRE?

A COMPUTE Regional Event brings together CS educators and researchers to strengthen the community, share experiences, and tackle regional challenges in computing education.

Workshops

Hands-on sessions for educators and researchers to develop their ideas.

Discussions

Group discussions on CS education challenges and solutions.

Networking

Connect with faculty and researchers from across the region.
